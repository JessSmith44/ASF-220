Hello fellow crafters and crochers! My name is Jessica, I prefer to go by Jess. I am a mother to 3, and an crafter. Crafting has been an amazing outlet for me, assisting in keeping anxiety at bay while being able to bring wonderful, fun, and beautiful things into the world. One of the greatest things about crafting to me is seeing the happiness and inspiration what I make can bring to others! Nothing quite compares to the smiles a hand crafted item can bring! Above all I just love to learn and enjoy life to the fullest.

Please feel free to leave a comment or share a pattern! If you run into any issues or have any questions you are welcome to reach out to me here as well as on our facebook page through the link in the navigation menu above.
